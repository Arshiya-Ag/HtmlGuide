The Diffrence Of em And i Tag

  • Tag em Ke Be Emphesis Eshare mikone Dar Jahayi Estefade Mishe Ke Ma Mikhahim Takid Bar Stress dashte Bashim Va Ba Gozahtan In Tag Mitonim In Mozo Ro Be Kasi Ke Code Maro Mikhone Neshon Bedim.

The Diffrence Of strong And b Tag

  • Tag b Tanha Baraye In ke tavajo Be in ghesmat Jazb She Estefade Mishe Va In Ghesmat Hamishe Daraye Ahamiat Ziad Nistesh.

  • Tag strong Yek Noe Tagi Hast ke Ba Inke Mesl b Bold mikonad Ama In Tag Alave bar Baraye Jalbe Tavajoh Neshan Dahande Ahamiat Balaye In Ghesmat Niz Hast.

    • EXAMPLE: WARNING Stop Immidetly

The Diffrence Of ul And ol Tag

  • Tag ul Ke Be Manaye UnOrdered List Hastesh Baraye In Estefade Mishe Ke Bekhahim Chand Gozine Ro Bedon Tartib Khasi List Konim. Ba Gozashtan Tag liPoshte Ghesmat Hayi ke List shode Ba tag Ye Noghte Gharar Migirad.

  • EXAMPLE:

    • My Hobbies

      • Playing games

      • Going Out

      • Traveling

  • Tag ol Ke Be Manaye Ordered Hastesh Mitone Ba Komak Tag li Yek Listi Ro Dorost Kone Ke Shomare Gozari shode Va Tartib Dare.

  • EXAMPLE:

    • My Three Favorite Series:

      1. Pianist

      1. Elite

      1. Peaky Blinders
